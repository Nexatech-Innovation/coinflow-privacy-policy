Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 09, 2026

At CoinFlow Inc. ("CoinFlow", "we", "our", or "us"), we are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and personal information. This comprehensive Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect your data when you access our mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the “Service”). By using CoinFlow, you consent to the practices described herein. We comply with applicable data protection laws, including those in Nigeria and international standards.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the CoinFlow Service. It details the types of information we gather, the purposes for which it is used, and your rights regarding your data. We encourage you to read this policy carefully and contact us with any questions.

Note: CoinFlow acts as a payment facilitator and does not store sensitive financial details like full card numbers or bank account information directly. All such data is handled by secure third-party processors.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide, improve, and secure our Service. This includes data you provide directly and information gathered automatically.

2.1 Personal Information

Information you voluntarily provide during registration, transactions, or support interactions:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth (for age verification)
  • Address or location details (if required for services)

2.2 Financial & Transaction Information

Data related to your use of payment features:

  • Wallet funding records and balances
  • Airtime, data bundles, and utility payment history
  • Transaction references, amounts, timestamps, and status
  • Payment method details (e.g., tokenized card info via processors)

2.3 Identity Verification Data

For compliance and security:

  • NIN (National Identification Number) and BVN (Bank Verification Number) search requests (processed securely and not stored beyond necessary)
  • Verification metadata, such as timestamps and results, to meet regulatory requirements
  • Biometric data if used for enhanced verification (e.g., facial recognition for KYC)

2.4 Technical & Usage Data

Automatically collected information:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., model, OS version, app version, unique device ID)
  • IP address, browser type, and approximate location (derived from IP)
  • Usage analytics (e.g., pages viewed, time spent, features used)
  • Crash logs, error reports, and diagnostic data
  • Cookies and similar technologies for session management

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information solely for legitimate purposes related to the Service:

  • To facilitate and process payments, wallet funding, and utility transactions
  • To verify identity, prevent fraud, and ensure compliance with anti-money laundering (AML) and know-your-customer (KYC) regulations
  • To provide customer support, resolve disputes, and communicate service updates or promotions (with opt-out options)
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve app functionality, user experience, and security
  • To personalize the Service, such as recommending features based on your activity
  • To comply with legal obligations, including audits and reporting to authorities
  • For internal research and development to enhance our products

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Our data processing is grounded in:

  • Consent: For optional features like marketing emails or personalized ads
  • Contractual Necessity: To deliver the Service you request, such as processing payments
  • Legal Compliance: To meet regulatory requirements in jurisdictions like Nigeria (e.g., CBN guidelines)
  • Legitimate Interests: For fraud prevention, security, and service improvements, balanced against your rights

5. Third-Party Services & Integrations

CoinFlow partners with reliable third parties to enable core functionalities. These entities may access limited data under strict agreements:

  • Payment gateways (e.g., Flutterwave, Paystack) for transaction processing
  • Analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase) for usage insights
  • Cloud providers (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud) for data storage and hosting
  • Security services (e.g., fraud detection APIs) to protect against threats
  • Identity verification providers for NIN/BVN checks

We ensure these partners adhere to data protection standards equivalent to ours. Please review their privacy policies for details.

6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Sharing occurs only when essential:

  • With service providers bound by confidentiality agreements
  • To comply with legal requests, subpoenas, or regulatory audits
  • To protect CoinFlow, users, or the public from harm or illegal activities
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale (with notice and safeguards)
  • Anonymized or aggregated data for research or business intelligence

7. Data Security

We employ industry-standard measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption (e.g., TLS/SSL for data in transit, AES for storage)
  • Access controls, firewalls, and regular security audits
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Incident response protocols for breaches (with prompt user notification where required)

Despite these efforts, no system is infallible. You are responsible for securing your account credentials.

8. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed:

  • Transaction records: Up to 7 years for compliance
  • Account data: While active, plus a reasonable period post-deletion
  • Usage logs: Up to 2 years for analytics

Data is securely deleted or anonymized when no longer required.

9. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights under laws like NDPR (Nigeria Data Protection Regulation):

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Erase data (subject to legal retention)
  • Restriction: Limit processing in certain cases
  • Portability: Transfer data to another service
  • Objection: Oppose processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent: For consent-based activities
  • Opt-Out: From marketing or cookies

To exercise these, contact us. We respond within 30 days.

10. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, pixels, and similar tools for:

  • Essential functions (e.g., session management)
  • Performance analytics
  • Personalization

You can manage preferences via browser settings or app options. Note: Disabling may limit functionality.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our Service is not for individuals under 18 (or 13 where stricter). We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If discovered, we delete it promptly.

12. International Data Transfers

Data may be transferred to servers in other countries (e.g., US, EU). We use adequacy decisions, standard contractual clauses, or other mechanisms to ensure protection.

13. Changes to This Policy
